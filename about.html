Our Story

Established in 2011, Furni-Collection (M) Sdn. Bhd. has emerged as a pioneering force in the outdoor furniture industry of Malaysia. Our journey began with a vision to revolutionize the way people experience outdoor living, driven by a deep-seated passion for craftsmanship and design excellence.


From humble beginnings, we embarked on a quest to create outdoor furniture that not only exceeded industry standards but also redefined the very concept of outdoor relaxation and entertainment. Drawing inspiration from nature's beauty and the evolving needs of modern lifestyles, we set out to craft furniture pieces that seamlessly blend form and function.


Over the years, our dedication to quality and innovation has propelled us to the forefront of the market, earning us a reputation for reliability, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Each piece in our extensive collection tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship, where every curve, joint, and finish is imbued with care and precision.


As we continue to grow and evolve, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering. We take pride in our Malaysian heritage, harnessing local talent and resources to create furniture that reflects the richness of our culture and the diversity of our landscapes.


Our journey is more than just about selling furniture; it's about inspiring outdoor experiences, fostering connections with nature, and creating cherished moments with loved ones. We believe that every outdoor space has the potential to be a sanctuary of comfort and beauty, and we are honored to play a part in bringing that vision to life for our customers.


With gratitude for the journey thus far and excitement for the road ahead, we invite you to join us as we continue to redefine outdoor living and create unforgettable moments under the open sky.
